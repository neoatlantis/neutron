NEUTRON: PROTONMAIL SECURITY ENHANCEMENT PLUGIN

You are seeing this page as Neutron, your firefox plugin for ProtonMail security enhancement, has detected a problem in your connection to ProtonMail. Therefore all accesses are blocked.


This usually means the browser have regarded this connection as insecure, or an untrustworthy certifcate in building the connection is used, which is potentially dangerous.

Since it's highly impossible that ProtonMail has misconfigured its server, this error may indicate someone or device is trying to spy your connection.

You have been warned. You should NOT try to overcome this problem by disabling Neutron. Instead, access ProtonMail again with VPN or an encrypted proxy software, or use Tor Browser.
